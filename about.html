ABOUT

Nana's kitchen has been a passion of mine since 2004 and operates in an at-home licensed kitchen. Through years of experience and experimentation we have came up with some fantastic products that will delight the family's taste buds and will cut preparation time down to a bare minimum.

COMPANY OVERVIEW

Nana's Comfort Foods was created in 2005 with only 4 mixes. They became an instant hit at craft fairs in the area. Since then the company has grown to over 18 dip, soup, and dessert mixes. I also have my own line of Meat Rubs and Seasonings. Our current goal is to create recipes using the existing product to make great tasting dishes and to improve upon the already great tasting ones offered. We are always up for suggestions on our path to make our kitchen feel like home!
